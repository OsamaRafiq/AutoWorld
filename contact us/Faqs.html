Frequently Asked Car Dealer Questions

At AutoWorld, we strive for the best in customer satisfaction. Our staff across all departments - from sales to service - is standing by to answer any and all questions you may have! No question is too big or too small.

FAQS

  • Why is taking a test drive so important?

    Buying a vehicle is a big purchase - you want to make sure you get it right. Taking a test drive is the best way to make sure you are happy with the vehicle's handling, braking, infotainment system, comfort, utility, functionality, and the overall driving experience.

  • Can I buy a car online?

    Yes, you can. AudtoWorld is pleased to offer online car buying! Start your vehicle search right here on our Inventory page. Without ever leaving home, you can shop for your next vehicle and get pre-approved all on our site. Contact us if you are interested in a vehicle, and we'll bring it to you for a test drive.

  • Who can I talk to about buying a car?

    A member of our sales team is standing by to speak with you about purchasing a car. To contact the sales department, you can fill out our online contact form, use our website chat feature, call or text sales at (021) 36630102, or stop by the showroom.

  • Where do I find specials on car sales?

    AutoWorld has some great sales going on. Finding our current specials is easy; they are listed right online under the Financing and Specials tab. Because specials change often, we recommend that you check back frequently. If you would like to take advantage of one of our specials, just give a call.

  • How will I know when my car is due for an oil change?

    Recommended oil change intervals vary between each make and model, but generally fall in the 'every 5000-8000 mile' category. If you are unsure, or would like some guidance on when to bring your car in, please give our service center a call at (021) 36630102

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    Any method of payments acceptable by you. For example: We accept MasterCard, Visa, PayPal, JCB Discover, Gift Cards, etc.
