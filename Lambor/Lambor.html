INTRODUCTION


HISTORY

Automobile Lamborghini S.p.A. is an Italian brand and manufacturer of luxury automobiles. Lamborghini's production facility and headquarters are located in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy. Italian manufacturing magnate Ferruccio Lamborghini founded the company in 1963.

EVOLUTION

Lamborghini has been the world's largest Automobile manufacturer since 1960, Lamborghini is famous for building world best sports cars reaching a production of 300 million by the end of 2019, as well as the world's largest manufacturer of internal combustion engines measured by volume, producing more than 12 million internal combustion engines each year. Lamborghini is one of the largest automobile manufacturer in the world.

VISION

From our past, we've learned perfection. This is how the first model of the Lamborghini was born. More performance, more control, more innovation. our cars are equipped with a powerful V10 engine and the latest technologies to perform at its absolute best.

DESIGN

Revolutionary thinking is at the heart of every idea from Automobile Lamborghini. Whether it is aerospace-inspired design or technologies applied to the naturally going beyond accepted limits is part of our philosophy.it advances every concept of performance, immediately establishing itself as the benchmark for the super car.
